| 1181-1218 CE | Golden Age | Jayavarman VII expands the empire to its greatest extent, builds Angkor Thom, Bayon temple, and 100+ hospitals |

## 7. Reward Game: Temple Builder

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Game Name** | Temple Builder |
| **Game Type** | Builder / Puzzle |
| **Reward Type ID** | `temple-builder` |
| **Celebration Message** | "You've built your own magnificent temple! The Khmer engineers would be proud of your creation! ğŸ›ï¸ğŸ˜âœ¨" |

**Description:** Players build their own Khmer-style temple complex by placing stone blocks, adding carved decorations, constructing a moat, and building a canal system to bring water. The game has stages:

1. **Foundation:** Place sandstone blocks to create the base pyramid (drag-and-drop)
2. **Towers:** Stack towers in the iconic Angkor Wat style (central tower tallest, 4 surrounding)
3. **Decorations:** Choose bas-relief carvings to add to the walls (mythological scenes)
4. **Water System:** Draw canals connecting your temple to a reservoir, then watch water flow through your system and rice paddies turn green
5. **Grand Reveal:** Zoom out to see your complete temple complex with animated people, elephants in procession, and monks chanting

**Key Learning Reinforcement:** The building process teaches kids about Khmer architecture, the importance of water engineering, and how temples were designed to represent cosmic mountains.
